About Me

I use Python and SQL to develop data models and algorithms that facilitate real-time analytics and support strategic business initiatives. My experience across various projects includes enhancing customer targeting in Adtech, optimizing transaction processing in banking systems, and enabling data-driven decision-making in retail operations.
